About "Rigged Hand":

With the Displacement modifier you can adjust the thickness of the hand, each finger can be controlled with only one bone per finger and that is a separate bone to clench a fist.Without subsurf modifier one hand has 512 faces, ideal for modern first person shooter, I think.All textures 2048px.http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZAP4TRZR76k[Hand Animation on Sketchfab](https://sketchfab.com/models/86dff766e51d4f2084a3768be7f08b8c)Some projects where this hand has found a use:[Blender Game Engine - Crysis-like Weapon attachment System](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=32PhM9D1Bco) by [Lars Pfeffer](https://www.youtube.com/user/uziulf)[First person basics ](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=JIxY0mmXqs0)by [Rocco martino ](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCrCw6UEKm4wF8_6RkWVrfFA)[The Island - [Open World Survival Game] (BGE) New Features](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NffyAjFO36o) by [blenderrendersky](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCoZ0-iWOJFElFJq8PlYaQTg)[Unmanned Vengeance](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nygovWQiFBo) short movie by[Harleynut97](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCfQgnXElklxLEsZltK3qagg)[Cyclesrender for a Cover](https://3dartdh.files.wordpress.com/2014/10/scixel-3d-hand-by-dennish2010.jpg?w=600&h=600)by[Scixel.es](http://www.scixel.es/)have fun with it and feel free to use this hand for your projects :)
